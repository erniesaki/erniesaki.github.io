Pilates

  • For more information or to book a place please contact Barbara Minshull Tel 07768067914 or visit www. healthybodiestraining.co.uk.

Chairman’s Stamp Appeal

May we take this opportunity to remind users of some of the Hall rules:

  • The cups, saucers, plates and cutlery are provided for the users use but if you use them please wash-up, dry and put them back in the cupboards neatly
  • There is a “no chewing gum” policy in the Hall - this is so that you are not inconvenienced by having chewing gum on the floors, carpets, tables and chairs
  • You are reminded that the playing of football with any type of ball is NOT permitted in the Hall
  • The Hall operates a zero tolerance policy with respect to violence and/or abusive behaviour by Hall users and/or their attendees towards its staff and reserves the right to refuse admission.
  • No staples or pins or any type are allowed to be used on any notice board or wall. They may also not be used to affix anything to the wooden doors or window frames in the hall
